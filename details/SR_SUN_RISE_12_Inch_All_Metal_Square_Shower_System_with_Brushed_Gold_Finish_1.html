SR SUN RISE 12-inch All Metal Square Shower System

Elevate your bathroom experience with the SR SUN RISE 12-inch all metal square shower system, designed for both new installations and updates. This premium set offers a perfect blend of modern aesthetics and long-term reliability, combining style and durability in one elegant piece.

Featuring a brushed gold finish that adds a touch of sophistication, the system is built to last and meet the U.S. Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC) standards.
